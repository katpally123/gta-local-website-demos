How visits work

A straightforward fitting flow.

  1. Call first. Confirm the garment, timing, and whether a fitting is needed.
  2. Bring the piece. Wear or bring the shoes and layers that affect the final fit.
  3. Review the work. Discuss the adjustment before the garment is marked or repaired.
  4. Pick up with confidence. Inspect the finished piece before it goes back into rotation.
