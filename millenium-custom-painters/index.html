Paint work should look sharp on day one and still hold up after daily life moves back in.

Every project starts with the same fundamentals: protect the space, prepare the surface, fix what needs attention, apply clean coats, and leave the room ready to use.

Process

A practical job flow that keeps the project moving.

  1. Walkthrough and quote. Discuss rooms, surfaces, colors, repairs, access, and timelines.
  2. Prep and protection. Cover floors, patch visible flaws, sand where needed, and set clean edges.
  3. Paint and review. Apply the right coats, check finish consistency, clean up, and complete a final walkthrough.
