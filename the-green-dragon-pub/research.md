## Why this business was selected

- Independent Toronto pub with strong review volume and a clear local reputation.
- Existing owned website is thin and dated: basic navigation, limited content depth, 2020 copyright, sparse conversion flow.
- Public sources provide enough verified information for a stronger production page without inventing facts.
- Distinct from the previous local project: pub/restaurant instead of salon, forest green/brass/red roadhouse mood instead of warm luxury salon.

## Verified public facts

- Current owned site: https://www.thegreendragonpub.com/
- Wanderlog: 4.5 rating with 674 reviews.
- Restaurantji: 4.4 rating with 190 ratings.
- Food96: 4.6 rating with 50 reviews.
- MapQuest / Tripadvisor snippet: 4.0 from 22 Tripadvisor reviews.
- Hours from owned site: Sunday to Wednesday 11 AM to 12 AM; Thursday to Saturday 11 AM to 1 AM.
- Publicly listed offerings include pub food, full bar, wings, takeout, patio/outdoor seating, game-watching atmosphere and monthly jam nights.
- The owned site says the pub has served more than 3 million wings and has been highlighted by BlogTO among Toronto wing spots.

## Review themes

- Wings are the main draw, especially crispy texture and sauce variety.
- Cozy neighborhood-pub atmosphere with regulars.
- Beer selection and casual sports-watching environment.
- Patio is appreciated in warm months.
- Staff are often described as friendly and welcoming.
