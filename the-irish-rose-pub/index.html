The room

A no-frills local with Irish pub bones.

The Irish Rose Pub sits on Weston Road in York, serving the area as a casual, familiar stop for beer, pub food, sports, and low-pressure nights out. Public descriptions call it a quaint Irish-style bar with British and Canadian beers and a compact menu of classic pub eats.

Review themes are direct and consistent: friendly staff, cozy atmosphere, cold Guinness, karaoke nights, local music, and a neighbourhood feel that keeps people coming back.
